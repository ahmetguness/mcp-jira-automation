Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of jujutsu sorcerers after he swallows a cursed object, the finger of the powerful curse Ryomen Sukuna. As he joins the Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical School, he learns to fight curses and protect humanity from supernatural threats, all while struggling with the consequences of his actions and the presence of Sukuna within him.
